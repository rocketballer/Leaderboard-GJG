Pool Rules

Selection Process

Each participant selects 12 player groups for the Masters Tournament.

Survival Mechanics

  • After Round 2 (the cut), we count how many of each participant's 12 player groups have at least 1 player who made the cut.
  • If fewer than 7 groups survive the cut, the participant is eliminated from contention.

Scoring

  • For participants with 7 or more surviving groups: Sum the best 7 scores to par from their surviving players.
  • For all participants: Include all surviving players' scores + missed-cut players' final scores.
  • The participant with the lowest total score (best to par) wins.
