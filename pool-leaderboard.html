Survival Rules

After Round 2 (the cut):

  • Count how many of each participant's 12 player groups have at least 1 player who made the cut.
  • If fewer than 7 groups survive, the participant is Eliminated.
  • If 7 or more groups survive, the participant remains Active.
Scoring System

Final scores are calculated as follows:

  • For participants with 7+ surviving groups: Sum the best 7 scores to par from their surviving players.
  • For all participants: Include all surviving players' scores + missed-cut players' final scores.
  • Lower scores are better (just like in golf).
